Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 20, 2026

Privacy First

1. Introduction

PadelKount is an iPhone and Apple Watch application designed to track padel match statistics, including heart rate data captured during gameplay.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. PadelKount is designed with a privacy-first approach: all data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Health & Fitness Data

PadelKount collects heart rate data via Apple Watch during your padel sessions. This data is:

  • Used solely to enrich your match statistics (e.g., average and peak heart rate per game)
  • Stored locally on your iPhone and/or Apple Watch
  • Never transmitted outside your device
  • Never shared with any third party

2.2 Game Statistics

PadelKount stores match history and game statistics (such as scores, session duration, and heart rate metrics) locally on your device. This data is not backed up to any cloud service operated by PadelKount.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

PadelKount does not collect any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or any account / login information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or usage telemetry
  • Any data transmitted to PadelKount servers (we operate none)

3. How We Use Your Data

All data collected by PadelKount is used exclusively to provide the app's core functionality — tracking and displaying your padel match statistics. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is described in this policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms, protected by your device passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, and iOS encryption.

PadelKount does not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure. There is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting your information.

5. Apple HealthKit

PadelKount accesses heart rate data through Apple HealthKit. In accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • Heart rate data is accessed only with your explicit permission
  • HealthKit data is used solely to display statistics within the app
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising or shared with third parties

You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time via iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → PadelKount.

6. Third-Party Services

PadelKount does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services. No data is shared with any third party.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access: you may request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure: delete all app data by uninstalling PadelKount or clearing app data in your device settings
  • Right to portability: your data is stored locally and accessible directly on your device
  • Right to object: revoke HealthKit permissions at any time via iOS Settings

Since all data is stored exclusively on your device and we operate no servers, the primary means of exercising these rights is through your device's own settings.

8. Children's Privacy

PadelKount is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of PadelKount after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
